How it works

Sign up

Choose your annual, daily or monthly pass on our website or through our mobile application.

Pick up your bike

First enter your user code and then enter your PIN code on the control panel to pick it up. You can associate your transport card.

Return correctly

Insert the bike into a free anchor, wait for "Return in progress", move the handlebar and confirm "Correct return" to finish your trip.

Who we are

Lima is the world's largest shared electric vehicle company. Our mission is to build a future where transportation is shared, affordable and carbon-free.
